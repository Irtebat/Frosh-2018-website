About Us

After going through all the pressure and putting in endless effort for the past two years, it’s time that it is well payed off.

Frosh Week presents one of the finest platforms to explore and showcase hidden talents and bury all forms of anxieties and stage fears, inculcating the traits of confidence and fortitude.

There are many problems which lead to insecurities and doubts and yes college life is to teach you how to manage all of this on your own but to cope with it from the beginning is not something so easy.

Frosh family is there to take care of the all these. We stand for No Ragging policy and healthy Junior - Senior interaction.
